About us

Our team is formed by developers from Myanmar. Our goal is to provide a centralised platform for information regarding flood disaster happening right now (Jul 2015/ Aug 2015). We collect information from any sources we can find both on the internet and the outside world. We also provide a platform for peer reporting of news.

Some of our volunteers are checking validity of the news but still we are in need of more volunteers.
